Emphasis on Plays

Plays allow SaturdayBall to calculate deeper stats that standard stat lines wouldn't be able to provide.

Enter in plays and sync them to your videos and highlight plays that you want to remember.

Leaderboards

Our stats go beyond FGM, REB and AST.

Advanced analytics such as True Passing %, True Shooting %, Def Rating and more are all easily calculated and avilable to your group.
